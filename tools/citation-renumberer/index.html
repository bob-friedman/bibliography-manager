Pro Citation Renumbering Tool

Interconvert numbered citations and short-form tags for easy editing.

Workflow:
1. Paste the manuscript text and numbered references into the boxes below.
2. Click 1. Convert Numbers to Tags. This replaces [1, 2] with [Smith 2020; Doe 2019].
3. Rearrange, add, or delete text in the manuscript as needed.
4. Click 2. Convert Tags to Numbers. This reassigns numbers chronologically and perfectly re-sorts the reference list! (Note: Unused references will be safely moved to the bottom of the list).
